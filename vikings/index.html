Minnesota Vikings

An underwhelming and often heartbreaking franchise

The Vikings have an all-time overall record of 517–442–11, the highest regular season and combined winning percentage among NFL franchises who have not won a Super Bowl, in addition the most playoff runs, division titles, and (tied with the Buffalo Bills) Super Bowl appearances (4).

The Vikings are a team designed to break your heart. But, have faith, this is our year! Do not let the 4-5 record fool you. The team is about to get hot and make a deep run. Or, at least I hope so.
